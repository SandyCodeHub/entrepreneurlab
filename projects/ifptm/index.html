  • What is IFPTM!
  • This is a place where existing team members of Preseed can bake their ideas with the advice from the Preseed team members and our network who will be willing to guide you through.
    Along the way you will have many lessons for having associated with us and in return for that we ask you to pledge 20% of your share in whatever initiatives you become a part of from here on.

    Read more


    If you agree to all that is indicated above please send us your Slide Deck at ifptm@preseed.in. If you do not have a Slide Deck please fill up the project detail form and someone from our network of collaborators will get into a dialogue with you. And please make sure you are a project with a 2 people team and a prototype. Also fill up the IFPTM Applicant Form.
